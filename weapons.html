Weapons Guide

Name Pros Cons Image Appears In Type Max Storage
Laser FREE. It is the startup weapon. Doesn't do much damage (at first). Levels up to max 4. Only one level in Descent 3. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Super Laser Better stats. In D2, pick it up to reach levels 5 and 6. In D3, Laser and Super Laser are separate. Slightly more energy consumption than normal laser. Descent 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Vulcan Fires rapidly. No energy use. Requires separate ammo. Medium damage. Descent 1 & 2 Primary Weapon 20,000 rounds
Gauss High damage. Fires multiple shots. Exploding bullets. No energy use. Requires vulcan ammo. Stand back to avoid self-damage. Descent 2 Primary Weapon 20,000 rounds
Vauss Vulcan combined with Gauss. Rapid and high damage. Uses its own type of ammo. Descent 3 Primary Weapon 12k (Phoenix), 15k (Pyro), 20k (Magnum)
Mass Driver Heavy shots with high damage. Hold fire to scope. Requires separate ammo. Descent 3 Primary Weapon Max 25 rounds
Napalm Fires streams of flame. Inflicts damage over time. Requires separate napalm ammo. Descent 3 Primary Weapon 120L (Phoenix), 150L (Pyro), 200L (Magnum)
Spreadfire Decent damage. Alternates horizontal and vertical 3-shot lines. Moderate energy consumption. Descent 1 & 2 Primary Weapon N/A
Helix More area than spreadfire. 5-shot rotating lines. High energy consumption. Descent 2 Primary Weapon N/A
Microwave Stuns and holds enemies in place. Low damage. Decent energy consumption. Descent 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Plasma Rapidly shoots bits of plasma. Consumes energy fairly fast. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Phoenix High damage. Ricochets off walls. Easy to kill yourself. Very high energy consumption. Descent 2 Primary Weapon N/A
EMD Gun Rapidly shoots many homing shots. Very high energy consumption. Descent 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Fusion High damage. Can be charged for more power. High energy use. High reload time. Overcharging damages your ship. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Omega Homing abilities. Steals shields from targets. D2 version damage is only OK and requires recharging. D3 version uses energy extremely fast. Descent 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Concussion Missile Moderately high damage. D1 & D2 versions move slowly. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 20 (D1/D2). 12-20 (D3)
Frag Missile Explodes into high-damage shrapnel. Use in large areas; stand back. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 12-20 (D3)
Homing Missile Homes on targets. Moderately high damage. D1 & D2 versions move slow. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 10 (D1/D2). 12-20 (D3)
Napalm Rocket Drops flaming napalm on impact. Don't burn yourself. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 5-15 (D3)
Impact Mortar Big explosion and very high damage. Slow reload and slow travel speed. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 7-15 (D3)
Smart Missile Explosion releases homing plasma bits. Stand back to avoid plasma. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 3-8 (D3). 5 (D1/D2)
Mega Missile Homes and creates massive explosion with extremely high damage. Stand far back. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 2-5 (D3). 5 (D1/D2)
Flash Missile Blinds enemies for a couple of seconds. Don't blind yourself. Descent 2 Secondary Weapon 20
Guided Missile Can be steered manually. Cannot be guided for longer than 30 seconds. Descent 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 10-20 (D3). 20 (D1/D2)
Cyclone Missile Splits into 6 small homing missiles. Don't fire at point blank range. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 7-15 (D3)
Mercury Missile Moves very fast. High firing rate. Only concussion missile level damage. Descent 2 Secondary Weapon 20
Earthshaker Missile Many large explosions with extremely high damage. Stand very far back. Descent 2 Secondary Weapon 10
Black Shark Missile Creates a mini black hole on impact. Stand back or you're toast. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 1-3 (D3)
Proximity Bomb Deals damage when touched by enemy (or you). Don't touch once dropped. Descent 1 & 2 Secondary/Countermeasure 10
Smart Mine Explodes with homing bomblets. Don't touch once dropped. Descent 2 Secondary/Countermeasure 10
Proximity Mine Bigger explosion and more damage than proximity bomb. You can take more damage if you touch it. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Seeker Mine Slowly moves toward enemies. Don't touch. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Bouncing Betty Bounces off walls all over the area. Don't get in its way. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Gunboy Functions as a small laser turret. Enemies can shoot these down. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Chaff Confuses and distracts homing missiles. Not actually a weapon. Don't bump into it. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
