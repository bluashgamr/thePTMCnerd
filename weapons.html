Weapons Guide

Name Pros Cons Image Appears In Type Maximum Quantity You Can Store(Only Applicable for Secondary Weapons and Countermeasures)
Laser FREE.It is the startup weapon. Doesn't do much damage(at first).However,the next time you get a laser, your laser levels up and has slightly better stats.You can level up to a max of laser level 4.However, there is only one level for the laser in Descent 3. Descent 1,2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Super laser Basically the normal laser with better stats.In Descent 2, when you pick this up, it boosts you to laser level 5, which is only possible with the super laser.Also in Descent 2,if you are already at laser level 5(in other words, you pick it up again), it boosts you to level 6,ther very highest laser.However, there is only one level of super laser in Descent 3.In Descent 2, The laser becomes the super laser.In other words,you only use the super laser.In Descent 3,The Laser and The Super Laser are seperate so you can use different ones at different times . Slightly more energy consumption than the normal laser. Descent 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Vulcan Fires shots rapidly.Does not use any energy.Fig. 1 shows the cannon, Fig. 2 shows the ammo clip containing 1,250 rounds. Requires seperate ammo.Does only a medium amount of damage. Descent 1 & 2 Primary Weapon 20000 rounds is the maximum.
Gauss Lots of damage per shot.Fires a bunch of shots at once.Exploding bullets.Does not use any energy.Fig. 1 shows the cannon, Fig. 2 shows the ammo clip containing 1,250 rounds. Requires separate ammo(the same ammo for the vulcan cannon).If you don't stand back when firing, you can take fairly high damage. Descent 2 Primary Weapon 20000 rounds is the maximum.
Vauss This is basically vulcan combined with gauss.It is very rapid like vulcan, and has pretty good damage like gauss. Uses it's own type of ammo(Not Napalm or Mass Driver ammo.). Descent 3 Primary Weapon 12000 rounds for the pheonix ship,15000 rounds for the pyro ship, and 20000 rounds for the magnum ship.
Mass Driver Fires heavy shots with high damage.Hold down the fire button to scope and release to fire. Requires seperate ammo(not vauss or napalm ammo). Descent 3 Primary Weapon The maximum rounds stored possible is 25.
Napalm Fires streams of flame setting enemies on fire, therefore inflicting more damage over time.Fires one stream of flame for the pheonix ship, 2 streams of flame for the pyro ship, and 3 streams of flame for the magnum ship. Requires separate napalm ammo(Not Vauss or Mass Driver ammo). Descent 3 Primary Weapon Maximum napalm ammo is 120.0 liters for the pheonix ship,150.0 liters for the pyro ship and 200.0 liters for the magnum ship.
Spreadfire A decent amount of damage.Shoots a line of 3 shots vertically, the horizontally,and alternates back and forth after that. Moderate energy consumption.Still has an OK amount of impact area. Descent 1 & 2 Primary Weapon N/A
Helix Has more impact area thant the spreadfire cannon.Shoots a line of 5 shots, then another line of 5 shots rotated 36 degrees counterclockwise and keeps rotating each line of 5 shots. High energy consumption. Descent 2 Primary Weapon N/A
Microwave Firing the microwave cannon at enemies keeps stunning them until you stop shooting, therefore holding them in place. Not a lot of damage itself, though.There's a decent amount of energy consumption. Descent 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Plasma Shoots bits of plasma very rapidly. Consumes energy fairly fast. Descent 1,2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Pheonix Does a lot of damage.Ricochets off walls.As rapid as the plasma cannon. You can easily kill yourself with it.Very high energy consumption. Descent 2 Primary Weapon N/A
EMD Gun Rapidly shoots many homing shots. Very high energy consumption Descent 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Fusion High damage.Hold the fire button to charge up to do even more damage.Release Fire button to shoot. High energy consumption.High reload time.Overcharging will damage your ship very quickly. Descent 1,2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Omega This cannon has homing abilities when you fire it.When you hit a target, it steals their sheild levels and gives it to you.There are 2 different versions of this cannon,one for Descent 2, and one for Descent 3.The version in Descent 3 has pretty good damage. Damage for the Descent 2 version of the omega cannon omega cannon is only OK.The Descent 2 version of the cannon starts at 100 charge which is the most you can have and uses up charge by firing,and must recharge to at least 40 charge before shooting again.With the Descent 2 version of the cannon, it takes an entire 5 energy to recharge from 0 to 100.The Descent 3 version however, does not have to recharge at all though,it uses your energy extremely quickly. Descent 2 & 3 Primary Weapon N/A
Concussion Missile Moderately high damage.May come in packs of 4.The Descent 3 version of this moves fast once fired. The Descent 1 & 2 version of this moves slowly when fired. Descent 1, 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 20 missles for Descent 1 & 2.In Descent 3 you have 12 missles for the pheonix ship(This has nothing to do with the pheonix cannon in Descent 2.),15 for the pyro ship(This has nothing to do with the Descent 3 flamethrower security robot who is a pyromaniac and I sure do hate it when he plays with napalm.), and 20 for the magnum ship(This has nothing to do with the gun.).
Frag Missile On impact, this missile explodes in to a bunch of moderately high damage pieces of shrapnel.May come in a pack of 4. Do it in a large area and stand back so you don't get hurt! Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 12 missiles for the pheonix ship, 15 missiles for the pyro ship, and 20 for the magnum ship.
Homing Missile This missile homes.Moderately high damage.May come in packs of 4.The Descent 3 version of this moves fast once fired. The Descent 1 & 2 version of this moves moderately slow once fired. Descent 1, 2 and 3 Secondary Weapon 10 missiles for Descent 1 & 2.In Descent 3 you have 12 missiles for the pheonix ship,15 missiles for the pyro ship, and 20 missiles for the magnum ship.
Napalm Rocket Fires a rocket that drops flaming napalm on impact. Be careful no to burn yourself! Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 5 rockets for the pheonix ship, 10 rockets for the pyro ship, and 15 rockets for the magnum ship.
Impact Mortar Fires a mortar that makes a big explosion and very high damage. Stand back to avoid big accidents.Slow reload rate.Moves slow once fired. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon Pheonix ship may hold up to 7 mortar shots,pyro can hold up to 10 mortar shots, the magnum can hold up to 15 mortar shots.
Smart Missile Firing this missle creates a moderately big explosion where you shot it,with the explosion blasting homing bits of plasma with decent damage. Make sure to stand back when firing just to make sure you don't get hit with plasma. Descent 1,2 & 3 Secondary Weapon 5 missiles is the max you can hold. Unless your in Descent 3 with the pheonix ship, and which makes it 3 missiles, or the Descent 3 magnum ship, which makes it 8 missiles.
Mega Missile When firing, your ship shoots a missile that homes and creates a large explosion with extremely high damage. Stand far back when firing to avoid major accidents. Descent 1, 2 and 3 Secondary Weapon 5 missiles is the max you can hold for Descent 1 & 2.In Descent 3, you can hold 2 missiles for the pheonix ship, 3 missiles for the pyro ,and 5 missiles for the magnum.
Flash Missile Explodes with moderately high damage and a blinding flash of light therefore blinding enemies for a couple of seconds.May come in packs of 4. Stand back.You don't want to get blinded as well,right? Descent 2 Secondary Weapon 20 missiles is the maximum you can store.
Guided Missile Once Fired, use the keys you use to turn,pull down, and pull up to guide the missile.Press the fire button again to make the missile home the rest of the way there.Moderately High damage.May come in a pack of 4. Cannot be guided for longer than 30 seconds. Descent 2 & 3 Secondary Weapon You can store up to 20 of these for the magnum ship in Descent 3 and Descent 1 & 2.There is a max of 10 for the pheonix ship and 15 for the pyro ship.
Cyclone Missile Blasts into 6 small homing missiles. Dont fire at point blank range.If you do you wont be hit by a missile, but 6 missiles. Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 7 missiles for the pheonix ship, 10 missiles for the pyro ship, and 15 missiles for the magnum ship.
Mercury Missile Moves fast once fired.Uses a very high firing rate.May come in a pack of 4. None except it only has as much damage as a concussion missile. Descent 2 Secondary Weapon You can store up to 20 of these.
Earthshaker Missile Explodes with many large explosions all with extremely high damage. Stand very far back,because you can easily kill yourself if you don't. Descent 2 Secondary Weapon 10 missiles is the maximum.
Black Shark Missile Creates a mini black hole on impact, sucks everthing in, and explodes all the stuff out with extremely high damage. Stand back or you're toast.Using invulnerability is recommended Descent 3 Secondary Weapon 1 missile for the pheonix ship, 2 missiles for the pyro ship, and 3 missiles for the magnum ship.
Proximity Bomb Press the drop bomb button or select it in your secondary weapon slot and press the fire secondary button to deploy.Deals moderately high damage when an enemy(or you) touches it.Fig.1 shows the bombs when you pick it up, Fig.2 shows it once dropped.May only come in packs of 4. For saftey reasons, don't touch the bombs once dropped. Descent 1 and 2 Classified both as countermeasure and secondary weapon 10 bombs is the maximum.
Smart Mine Press the drop bomb button or select it in your secondary weapon slot and press the fire secondary button to deploy.Deals moderately high damage when an enemy(or you) touches it.Fig.1 shows the mines when you pick it up, Fig.2 shows it once dropped.Explodes with homing bomblets.Only comes in packs of 4. For saftey reasons don't touch the mines once dropped. Descent 2 Classified both as countermeasure and secondary weapon 10 mines is the maximum.
Proximity Mine Basically, a proximity bomb with a bigger explosion and more damage. Unfortunatly that can also mean you can take more damage(if you touch it). Basically looks like a proximity bomb. Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Seeker Mine This is like a proximity bomb and proximity mine, except when deployed it slowly moves toward enemies. You guessed it, don't touch. A seeker mine looks a lot like a smart mine. Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Boucing Betty This is a mine that when deployed, it bounces off the walls and goes all over the place in the area. Don't get in its way. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Gunboy Press the drop bomb button to deploy.Functions as a small laser turret that shoots enemies in all directions. Don't completely rely on it(or even a couple of these sometimes).Enemies can shoot these down moderately quickly. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
Chaff Confuses and distracts homing missiles. Hee Hee Hee!I lied to you.This isn't actually a weapon.However, it fits best at this part of the website.Be Carful not to bump in to it!Hard to destroy once deployed. No Image Descent 3 Countermeasure Unknown
